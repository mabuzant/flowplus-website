Brand Guidelines

Version 2.0 · June 2026 · Abu Dhabi, UAE

01

Logo

The flow+ wordmark is the sole primary identifier — a custom geometric lowercase treatment with a rounded plus. The plus is part of the name and is never separated, recolored, or reordered. Whenever the name is set in live text, “flow” is thin (300) and “+” is bold (700).

flow+
flow · weight 300  |  + · weight 700
On void · primary
On elevated surface
On frosted mesh
Do

Keep clear space equal to the height of the “+” on all sides. Minimum width 120px digital / 30mm print. Always white on a dark surface.

Don't

Never render it as “+flow” in Arabic/RTL — lock it with bidi isolation. Never set “flow” bold or “+” thin. Never distort, rotate, recolor, outline, or place on light backgrounds.

03

Frosted Mesh Gradients

The signature of the brand. Five fixed palettes, each rendered as frosted random-shape gradients — built only from radial and conic gradients, heavily blurred, with a noise grain overlay. Never a linear gradient. Gradients appear only on main card elements, buttons, and showcases — never on backgrounds or small UI.

Recipe
3–4 radial-gradients at random positions
+ 1 conic-gradient sweep
+ blur(44px) saturate(135%)
+ 42% noise grain overlay
Do

Use 3–4 color stops from one palette. Always blur heavily and add the grain overlay so the gradient reads as frosted, never solid. Place a dark scrim under any text.

Don't

Never use a linear gradient. Never use a flat two-tone blend. Never mix two palettes in one element. Never apply mesh to backgrounds or tiny components.

Effects & Motion

Frost glass, grain, a noised & distorted black background, fluid morphing gradient blobs, floating gravity, and scroll-driven zoom. Motion is smooth and weighted — never bouncy or playful.

Reveal content with a scale-up zoom on scroll. Let cards lift with gravity. Keep background orbs drifting slowly for depth.

10

Do's & Don'ts

The hard rules that protect the brand on every surface.

Do

Always dark backgrounds (#000–#0D0D10). Text always white or muted white.

Don't

Never a white or light background. No light mode, ever.

Do

Use the five frosted mesh palettes (3–4 stops, radial + conic, blurred, grained) on main cards only.

Don't

Never a linear or two-tone gradient. Never a solid flat accent fill.

Do

Lead with big type and scale. Lock flow+ with bidi isolation so it never reverses.

Don't

Never render “+flow”, and never set “flow” bold or “+” thin. No emojis or decorative icons — partner tool logos are the only exception.

Do

Tint photography black and reveal it on scroll. Use fluid morphing gradients and gravity float for depth.

Don't

No drop shadows on cards — use glass glow. No mirror reflections under cards. No bouncy or spinning motion.
